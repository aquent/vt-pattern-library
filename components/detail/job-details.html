        <p>Our client in Detroit is seeking an in-house Sr. Creative Director to join their team!</p>

            <li>5+ years in graphic design with a strong digital portfolio</li>
            <li>5+ years in art direction or creative role for websites and ecommerce</li>
            <li>5+ years responsive email design experience</li>
